The advent of the "information age", as theorized by science fiction, has promised to transform the world into something completely new, guided by technological and transhumanist principles. As we move into the future, aka the 21st century, we find our culture continually suprised by how new inventions both resemble yet subvert science fiction. We do not have hoverboards, but we have social media networks that allow individuals to exist and transform themselves in an entirely digital realm. Old definitions of what constitutes "reality", the physical bedrock upon which we stand, grows thinner and thinner as more and more of life is lived outside our physical body and inside the internet.

New Genesis Artform is a creative project aimed at exploring how the digital world may be used for spiritual action by inverting and augmenting status-quo means of living.
